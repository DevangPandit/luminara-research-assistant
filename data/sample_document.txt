Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and the former captain of the India national cricket team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time. Kohli holds numerous records, including being the highest run-scorer in T20 Internationals and the fastest to reach 10,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs). He plays for Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and for Delhi in domestic cricket.

As of 2024, Kohli has broken many of Sachin Tendulkar's records, including surpassing him for the most centuries in ODI cricket. He is known for his aggressive playing style, exceptional fitness regimen, and remarkable consistency across all three formats of the game.

Kohli made his international debut for India in August 2008 against Sri Lanka in an ODI. He was part of the Indian squad that won the 2011 Cricket World Cup, and he captained the team that won the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.

He is married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma. Outside of cricket, Kohli is heavily involved in philanthropy through the Virat Kohli Foundation, which aims to support underprivileged children.
